Basic environmentThe principles of construction

PRINCIPLE

Tong Fang Yun through the existing land use planning in-depth study, full respect for customer recommendations based on the new design concept to create a dynamic data center. In order to build a secure, sustainable, data center with industry agglomeration and international influence.

Standard

National and local engineering design standards and standards of the international standard THE UPTIME INSTTTUTE data center level standard telecommunications industry standard national standard electronic information system room design specifications -GB50174- 2017 information security, disaster backup, security, intelligent building, fire lighting, integrated wiring, video security, fire alarm, power supply grounding, air conditioning environment and other requirements, standards and guidelines.

Safety

The establishment of security levels for efficient and tight security area in accordance with the safety level to establish a safe partition, according to the purpose and number of traffic lines, in accordance with the safety partition to select the security function, multi-layer protection system, perimeter, public area, room module. Fire, water, anti-theft, grounding, lightning protection, anti-rat and other animals, dust, ground load capacity, anti-electromagnetic interference, anti-dry sound and other aspects to take effective measures. The functional area of the engine room is relatively independent, reducing the scope and proportion of personnel access.

Reliability

Select the appropriate data center layout planning, improve fault tolerance, disaster recovery, redundancy, select the right place to avoid the occurrence of disasters to establish a high standard of structural buildings, structural design, power distribution design, air conditioning design, equipment selection And other aspects of the high reliability of the design and construction. Based on the design of standardized disaster recovery system, the use of relevant software technology to provide a strong management mechanism, control means, accident monitoring and security and other means of security, infrastructure design to fully consider the disaster resistance of infrastructure construction, natural disasters and environmental interference Have a high strength of resistance.

Manageability

Comprehensive and perfect room management and monitoring system, the use of advanced management and monitoring system equipment and software to achieve advanced centralized management and monitoring, real-time monitoring, testing and recording the operation of the entire room to quickly determine the failure to reduce the maintenance workload of the engine room management , In advance to consider the equipment replacement and maintenance of the necessary space and backup.

Flexibility and scalability

Taking into account the current needs and business development, good capacity planning to ensure that the power supply, the area of the reservation in line with future development needs, the use of modular design, easy to replace, upgrade and expansion of equipment, with a variety of network transmission, a variety of physical interfaces Ability. Using elevated floor decoration.

Practicality and advanced nature

The use of advanced technology to establish a fully embodied energy saving, environmental protection, emission reduction of green data center, to create a comfortable space, the establishment of a convenient operation and maintenance systems and man-machine interface, any equipment can be maintained under the conditions of operation can not be maintained , Excellent PUE, can be staged construction
